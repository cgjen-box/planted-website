We believe meat doesn't have to come from animals. It just has to taste incredible.

That's why we started Planted. To create plant-based meat so good, even the biggest meat lovers can't tell the difference.

It started with a question

What if you could enjoy the taste and texture of meat without the environmental impact? What if you could have your steak and eat it too?

In 2019, four friends in Zurich asked themselves this question. They were food scientists, engineers, and entrepreneurs who loved meat but couldn't ignore the facts: animal agriculture is one of the biggest contributors to climate change.

So we built something better

We didn't want to make another veggie burger that tastes like cardboard. We wanted to create something that would actually satisfy a meat craving. Something with the same fibrous texture, the same sizzle in the pan, the same satisfaction on the plate.

Using cutting-edge food science and good old Swiss engineering, we developed a proprietary process that transforms simple plant proteins into meat-like structures. The result? Plant-based meat that even butchers have trouble identifying.
